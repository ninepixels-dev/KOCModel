Detailed Design (PGS) Field Spacing


Project Execution (Phase 1): Detailed Engineering & Design Field Spacing Considerations

The results of the field spacing assessment for facility siting, layout, segregation, and equipment spacing obtained during the FEED stage of the project shall be re-assessed during Detailed Engineering (PGS 4.1) and adjusted as necessary using a full developed QRA, if so determined necessary by the KOC Controlling Team.

If the full developed QRA is needed, the end user shall apply the rule sets as given in the Guidelines, Section 10.1.
