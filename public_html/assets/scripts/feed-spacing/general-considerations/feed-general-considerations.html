FEED General Considerations


This section relates to the field spacing arrangements during FEED stage of the project (PGS Gate 3.1). The intent here is to allow the end user to arrive at appropriate field spacing arrangements during FEED.

Before commencing field spacing for all project stages (and in particular PGS 2.1, 3.1 and 4.1), the end user should consider the general Safety Engineering Consideration as given in the Guidelines, Part B.

Additional references to the pertinent sections in the Guidelines which should be reviewed by the end user for field spacing during FEED (PGS 3.1) are as follows:

  1. FEED Siting Basis and Implications (Guidelines Section 6.1)
  2. FEED Siting Considerations (Guidelines Section 6.2)
  3. FEED Layout and Segregations Implications (Guidelines Section 7.1)
  4. FEED Layout and Segregation Considerations for different Facility Areas (Guidelines Section 7.2)
  5. FEED Equipment Spacing Implications (Guidelines Section 8.1)
  6. FEED Equipment Spacing Considerations (Guidelines Section 8.2)
  7. Process to Determine the Distances for Detailed FEED (Guidelines Section 9)
  8. Rule Sets for Determination of Distances for FEED Field Spacing (Guidelines Section 9.1)
  9. Models for Determination of Distances for FEED Field Spacing (Guidelines Section 9.2)
