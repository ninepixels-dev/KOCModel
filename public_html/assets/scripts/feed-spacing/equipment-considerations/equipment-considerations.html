FEED Equipment Spacing Considerations


The implications of equipment spacing are similar to the implications in layout and segregation, in that the principal consideration is prevention of escalation of a major accident from one item of equipment to another due to insufficient physical isolation between the items of equipment.

A detailed set of FEED Spacing Considerations is provided in the Guidelines, Section 8 and is not repeated here.

Further information in relation to these aspects is given in the Guidelines, Section 8.
