Kuwait Oil Company - Field Spacing Tool



This application presents the FIELD SPACING TOOL for Kuwait Oil Company (KOC) onshore facilities and is designed to be used in conjunction with the KOC Field Spacing Guidelines.

The FIELD SPACING TOOL provides for detailed consideration and determination of parameters to achieve safe facility siting, layout, segregation, equipment spacing and building risk assessment of KOC onshore facilities during the major stages of the KOC Project Gate System (PGS) Process, as defined in the KOC Procedure for the Preparation of Project HSE Plan.

The PGS is the governing framework used by KOC for selecting, planning and managing capital projects (that meets specific criteria) in a consistent and disciplined manner throughout the Project lifecycle.

This tool is, in particular, related to the Concept Selection (PGS 2.1), Front End Engineering Design (FEED) (PGS 3.1), and Detailed Engineering and Design (PGS 4.1) project stages.

Making safe arrangements for Field Spacing of KOC onshore facilities depends on consideration of all aspects of the Guidelines.

It is of vital importance that the End User familiarise him/herself with the provisions of the Guidelines before commencing the facility field spacing.
